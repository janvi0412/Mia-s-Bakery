Our Story

"Started with a tiny oven, a borrowed recipe, and a stubborn love for the smell of fresh bread at dawn. Early mornings meant flour everywhere, burnt batches, and learning patience one loaf at a time. Word spread through warm smiles, honest flavors, and treats that felt like home.

What began as a small neighborhood bake turned into a daily ritual for many. Today, we’re still baking with the same heart, just with a lot more trays in the oven.""
